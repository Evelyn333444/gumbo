Top 3 fine-dining restaurants in Providence, Rhode Island.

1. Bacaro

Bacaro is a cozy Italian restaurant located in the heart of Providence.
In addition to its delicious food, they also have an excellent selection of wines.

2. Hemenway's

Hemenway's is a seafood restaurant that offers a wide variety of fresh seafood, including shellfish, chowder, and a variety of fish dishes.

3. Fleming's

Fleming's is a steakhouse located on the ground floor of the Omni Providence Hotel.
They offer a variety of steak cuts, as well as seafood, caviar, and an excellent wine list.
